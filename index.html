Dean “F.R.E.A.K” Farmer (RIAA® Certified Multi Platinum Producer) is an American Record Producer/Multi-Instrumentalist/Artist and now EDM DJ born in Chicago, Illinois. He is well known for Producing 50 Cent’s #1 Billboard smash single “Amusement Park” with Dangerous LLC from the 13 X Platinum album “Curtis”. F.R.E.A.K also Co-Produced and contributed his bass, keyboard and guitar talents on Jay Z’s “Say Hello” Produced with DJ Toomp for the Billboard 5X Platinum #1 album, “American Gangster” in addition to making contributions on projects for Michael Jackson , Roger Sanchez , Madonna , Prince , Janet Jackson to name a few. F.R.E.A.K spent many years producing, writing, and arranging music with the world renowned house music DJ Roger Sanchez and his creative genius has made a vital contribution to Sanchez’s success. So after years of studying the dance music world from behind the scenes, F.R.E.A.K has now chosen to take the leap into this constantly evolving genre of music as an EDM DJ.
